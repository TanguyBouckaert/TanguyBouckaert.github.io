Who am I?

Hello, I'm Tanguy Bouckaert, a passionate developer and game designer with a strong technical basis in web development and independent game production. My journey started during my studies at HoGent, where I specialized in web development, Java programming, database management and mobile app development (both for Android and iOS). This experience gave me the opportunity to gain practical knowledge in building websites, writing Java applications and creating fully functional web and mobile applications.

To further expand my skills, I continued my studies at Howest in the field Independent Game Production via the Digital Arts and Entertainment Program. Here I learned to design games and create 3D models with Blender and Maya, and fully finished developing games with the Unity engine. This mix of technical and creative skills sets me up to be capable of making engaging and interactive games.

In addition to my academic career, I am an active volunteer at my local youth movement, where I functioned as a youth coordinator for six years and then for two years as head of the movement. In this leadership role I was responsible for planning activities, managing teams and shaping the direction of the organization. This experience has given me valuable teamwork, communication and leadership skills that I use in my professional work.

When I'm not programming or making games, you can find me running or on the golf course—activities that keep me focused, disciplined and will always push me to my limits.

I'm always open to new challenges and collaborations, so don't hesitate to get in touch!
